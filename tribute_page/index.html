Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

1630-1680

Founder of the Maratha kingdom of India

Shivajiraje Bhosale was born in the Shivneri Fort in Maharashtra on 19 February 1630 to mother Raajmata Jijabai and father Shri. Shahajiraje Bhosale. It is said that mother Jijabai prayed to Lord Shiva in Shivneri Fort for a brave son and kept her son name as Shivaji after the Hindu god Shiva.Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a devout Hindu ruler of India. He established the Maratha Empire in western India.He had taken both religious and war education since childhood.Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was married to Saibai.He became "Chhatrapati" as soon as he was coronated in Raigad.During his reign, he had to struggle a lot with the Mughal emperor Aurangzeb.He ruled the Maratha Empire in India from 1674 to 1680.Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj passed away on 3rd April 1680.
